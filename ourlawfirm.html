Our Services

Gelegenheit covers the full spectrum of legal services important to businesses in today’s global marketplace, the law firm also assists nonprofit organizations. We focus on the following areas of practice:

CORPORATE & COMMERCIAL

Our practice is centered on transactional and legal advisory services concerning day-to-day business transactions, regulatory issues and corporate governance affairs. Among others, we take care of the legal aspect of all kinds of commercial transactions, we help our clients establish their businesses in Rwanda and take care of their legal issues throughout their business journey. Also, we advise clients on restructuring, mergers and acquisitions as well as formation of joint ventures. Gelegenheit aims not to be only your legal adviser, but to stand shoulder to shoulder in all your business legal requirements.




BANKING AND FINANCE

Lenders and borrowers face a lot of challenges, including legal and business related. The good news is that some of our Attorneys have been bankers themselves. Additionally, with our experience representing lenders and borrowers, and handling other different banking and finance transactions, Gelegenheit is exceptionally well positioned to help you close all your banking and finance transactions.

CAPITAL MARKET

We offer comprehensive legal support on a variety of offerings in the domestic and international capital markets. Domestically we represent and advise entities that issue securities to raise capital and security holders seeking to sell their securities. Our Attorneys have comprehensive understanding of the national securities laws administered by Rwanda Capital Market Authority as well as the rules of Rwanda Stock Exchange.


REAL ESTATE

We advise clients with regard to the entire spectrum of their real estate-related needs, from land acquisition and use to development and construction, acquisitions, dispositions, financing and leasing as well as real estate management. Our clients benefit from other practice areas of our firm such as banking and finance, tax as well as litigation.

TAX

Our Attorneys have handled some of the most complex tax issues at other law firms and other business places where they worked before. There is no doubt that the depth and breadth of our legal and market knowledge guarantees our capabilities to support your business with advanced tax solutions in the increasingly challenging business environment. We advise a wide spectrum of clients on tax, whether private sector, institutional and multi-national organizations as well as foreign investors.




ENERGY, MINING & INFRASTRUCTURE

The Energy, Mining & Infrastructure sectors in Rwanda and on the continent are undergoing a major transformation. At Gelegenheit, we understand what that means for all the players. We understand the industry and we know who the players are and what the issues are. We know how to help you make a difference environmentally while still maintaining your business objectives. Our transactional capability, together with our wide-ranging expertise in finance, corporate, tax, environment and government contracts means we can provide commercially suitable solutions to your energy, mining and infrastructure projects.

EMPLOYMENT AND LABOR

Gelegenheit college of law offers a full range of employment and labor law services. Our employment and labor attorneys are well-known for their innovative and business oriented legal solutions. We assist local and multinational institutions with respect to the full range of employment-related issues, including employment contracts, handbooks and policies, business restructuring and trainings. The interface between the Firm's employment and labor practice and other important areas of our practice, such as tax, particularly makes us the lawyers of first choice for employment and labor issues.



NGO & INGO

Gelegenheit helps Non-Governmental Organizations (NGOs) and International Non-Governmental Organizations (INGOs) register in Rwanda. We help them prepare all the legal documents required for registration and renewal of their licenses, and we advise them on their daily activities.

WHY GELEGENHEIT?

Succeeding in today’s ever-changing markets requires in-depth understanding of the country’s culture, economy, laws and politics. At Gelegenheit, we understand that our clients come to us because they need a capable legal expert who can take care of their legal issues, so that they can concentrate on other aspects of their businesses.

Our lawyers have comprehensive knowledge and understanding of the law and the markets, we understand well the political, economic and cultural factors at play, and the way national and international legal frameworks operate and cooperate. This gives us the capacity to offer a wide range of services to suit the needs of local and multinational corporations as well as business enterprises of all kinds.

Unlike most transactional law firms, we dedicate enough time getting to know our clients and ensuring that we fully understand their legal issues. We also limit the number and type of matters we take on so that we can be able to deliver the best service to our existing clients. When you work with us, we will truly listen to you, and will make sure that you understand all of your options and what we believe to be the best suitable solution to your legal issues.

Our team is dedicated to excellence, and we pride ourselves on holding true to this pledge. We understand that you need a lawyer you can trust to provide you with the best legal advice, work for your best interests, and can deliver on time.

Mr Jean Pierre Tuyishime | Profile


Jean Pierre is an Advocate of the High Court of Rwanda and a member of the East African Law Society who practices law under FixLex Law Chambers as the Managing Partner.

Prior to joining FixLex Law Chambers, Jean Pierre served at CERTA LAW as Attorney. Before the call to the Bar, he served as Senior Legal Officer at EQUITY BANK RWANDA PLC and beforehand was an in-house lawyer at ACCESS BANK RWANDA PLC. Besides legal practice, he teaches Law at various Universities in Rwanda and is a Fellow of the Young African Leaders Initiative (YALI).

Jean Pierre holds a Master’s Degree in Law (LLM) from the University of Dar es Salaam, Tanzania where he focused on East African Community Law and Law of Regional Economic Integration. He is a holder of a Postgraduate Diploma in Legal Practice (DLP) from ILPD and a First Class Bachelor’s Degree in Law (LLB) from Kigali Independent University, Rwanda. His practice areas include corporate and commercial, banking and finance, tax, capital market, as well as EAC law.

He is a native speaker of Kinyarwanda, has excellent command of English and French as well as a working knowledge of Swahili and basic German.
